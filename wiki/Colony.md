# Colony Building RP

Here you still follow most of the rules of nomads save for all the
faction warring etc. since they can't happen. This is an HRP map, grey
tiding and meming shittery will not be tolerated. There are an
assortment of jobs available, however don't choose an important one if
you're just gonna go SSD after 5 minutes. Thats how you get job banned.
Here's a table of these jobs.

The higherups, those responsible for their respective departments, with the Governor as head. Each commander has authority over their own department and a say in what happens within them. They must work together with the Governor in order to ensure colonial prosperity.

The banker oversees colonial funds, taxation, and wages, necessitating consultation for fiscal decisions. The Colonial Bank is the only legal and registered financial entity within the colony. The members of this financial entity are tasked with keeping the colonial funds safe and making the colony money, as well as managing expenses, taxes, wages, etc.
